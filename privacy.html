Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 03 January 2024

The 'Productivity Tracker' mobile application (the "App") is committed to safeguarding user privacy, including children under the age of 13. This Privacy Policy outlines the App's policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

The App records a table of start and stop times locally on your device when you press the start and stop buttons, respectively. This data is generated by the App and is stored on your device. The App does not transmit this data to any external server.

Data Storage

All data generated by the App, including start and stop times, is stored locally on your device. The App has access to this data solely for the purpose of providing its intended functionality and does not share it with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App can be used by children under the age of 13. While the App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, it is important for parents or guardians to be aware that the App generates and stores data locally on the device. If you have any concerns or questions about the data stored by the App, please contact us for further information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

The App may update its Privacy Policy from time to time. Users are encouraged to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after being posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions about the Privacy Policy or the data stored by the App, please contact us at feedback.vitan@gmail.com.
